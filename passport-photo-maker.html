Official Guidelines for Online Passport and Visa Photos

When applying for national passports or international visas (like the US Visa or Schengen Visa), the background, face composition, and file size restrictions must strictly adhere to the guidelines. Failing to upload a compliant photo will lead to application rejections.

Backdrop Guidelines

Most competitive exams and visa applications require a pure white background. Yellow backgrounds or patterns are strictly prohibited. Always use our built-in backdrop replacement to set it to white or light blue.

Image Scaling & DPI

Official passport sizes demand 300 DPI high-quality outputs. Our engine automatically rescales your images to standard dimensions like 350x450 pixels (3.5x4.5 cm) or 600x600 pixels (2x2 inches) while keeping quality crisp.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the official size of an Indian Passport photo in pixels?

The standard size is 35mm x 45mm, which converts to 350x450 pixels when rendered at 300 DPI. The background color must be white, and the face must cover 70-80% of the photo.

What are the photo dimensions for a US Visa online application?

A US Visa photo must be square (2 inches x 2 inches or 5.1 cm x 5.1 cm). In digital pixels, it is 600x600 px (minimum) to 1200x1200 px (maximum), with a target file size under 240 KB.

Why does the background color matter?

Automated facial recognition scanners used by passport offices and immigration portals require consistent light, solid backgrounds to accurately map facial features. Photos with busy backdrops are automatically rejected.
