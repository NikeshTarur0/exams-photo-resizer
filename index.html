Frequently Asked Questions & Exam Guidelines

How do I resize a photo to 20 KB - 50 KB for SSC CGL / CHSL?

Select SSC CGL / CHSL from the dropdown menu, drag and drop your photo into the passport workspace, adjust zoom if necessary, and click download. Our engine automatically adjusts JPEG compression quality to keep the final file between 20 KB and 50 KB.

What are the photo requirements for UPSC Civil Services IAS?

UPSC requires passport photos to be between 20 KB and 300 KB with minimum 350x350 pixel dimensions. The photo must be clear with a light or white background, showing 3/4th face view.

Is my photo stored on any external server?

No. Your privacy is 100% guaranteed. All image cropping, scaling, and file compression take place locally on your own device browser using modern HTML5 Canvas technology.

Why do online exam portals reject uploaded photos?

Most rejections occur due to incorrect aspect ratio (distorted faces), dark backgrounds, or exceeding strict file size bounds (e.g., photo > 50 KB). Our real-time compliance meter alerts you before downloading.
