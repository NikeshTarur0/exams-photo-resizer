Guidelines for Converting PDF Pages to JPG/PNG

Some portals require supporting certificates (educational transcripts, work experience documents, ID proofs) to be uploaded as JPG files rather than multi-page PDFs. Our PDF-to-image tool renders each page on an HTML5 canvas to guarantee high resolution outputs.

In-Browser Extraction

We use pdf.js compiled scripts to execute PDF rendering locally. Your confidential certificates and documents are never exposed to remote web servers.

Page-By-Page JPG Generation

Each page is extracted as a separate high-resolution image file. You can preview all pages side-by-side and choose which ones to download.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a page limit for PDF extraction?

No. Our local JS parser can render PDFs of any page count, limited only by your device's available RAM.

Will the extracted images look blur?

No. We render the PDF pages at a high zoom scale ratio (typically 2.0x scale) to ensure all text characters remain sharp and readable.
