About Exams Photo Resizer

Welcome to Exams Photo Resizer, India's leading fast online image resizing utility designed specifically for competitive exam candidates.

Our Mission

Every year, millions of Indian students apply for major recruitment examinations including UPSC Civil Services (IAS/IPS), SSC CGL, SSC CHSL, IBPS PO/Clerk, NTA NEET (UG), NTA JEE Main, SBI PO, GATE, and Railway RRB NTPC. Each exam board imposes strict parameters regarding image dimensions (width/height in pixels) and file size (min/max KB limits).

Our mission is to empower candidates by providing a 100% free, 1-click instant resizer engine that automatically scales photos and digital signatures to comply strictly with official examination guidelines without compromising visual quality.

Key Features & Technology

  • Pre-loaded Official Specifications: Integrated rules for major national and state recruitment bodies.
  • Privacy First Engine: HTML5 Canvas browser processing guarantees your sensitive personal photos remain private on your device.
  • Real-Time Size Validator: Interactive compliance bar verifying minimum and maximum KB size bounds.
  • Mobile & Desktop Friendly: Fully responsive web application optimized for smartphones, tablets, and desktop PCs.
