Frequently Asked Questions

How can I compress my signature to less than 20 KB?

Upload your signature scan, set the "Target Max File Size" field to 20 KB (or click the quick "20 KB" pill), and our engine will find the perfect quality scale to keep it under 20 KB instantly.

Will my image quality drop during compression?

We preserve pixel dimensions. Only the high-frequency color artifacts that are invisible to the human eye are compressed. This guarantees that text strokes and faces remain clear and eligible.

Can I compress PDF documents here?

This tool is optimized for photos (JPG, PNG, WEBP). To extract pages from a PDF to JPG, use our dedicated "PDF to Image" tab in the suite navigation.
